Design Works

Steve Kurzman

Principal User Experience Designer at SAP Concur

In many ways, Xi was an ideal design intern: she has a great combination of product design and motion design skills, and seemed to intuitively grasp fairly complex design problems with little supervision. She contributed a lot to our team by working on both new feature projects and our nascent style guide/design pattern library. On the former, she was very adept at coming up with a variety of design solutions and iterating on them based on feedback from colleagues and stakeholders. On the latter, Xi displayed a real talent for innovation by creating a clear way to spec motion design patterns for our engineers. Overall, Xi was a pleasure to work with, and added a lot of value in a short amount of time. Any product or UX team would be lucky to have her.

Kat Angeles

Visual Designer at SAP Concur

Xi was an absolute pleasure to work with and we all miss her and her bright energy very much! As our summer intern, Xi laid the foundation for our design system's motion design section—gaining a working expertise of Google's material design specs for motion and incorporating them into our app to improve the overall user experience as well as create thoughtful little details to add delight. Xi was an ease to mentor as she is not only a fast learner but a self starter and required very minimal direction. Her familiarity with UX principles and her strong eye for visual design lead her to quickly turn around a large number of projects in a short amount of time. I strongly believe that she would be a valuable asset for any design team.
