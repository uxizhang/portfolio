Calendar note

A user research for class project found that users were reluctant to go back and forth between calendar and events to check different day’s events. Combining these two views in the same page, the experience for users to check and edit events can be intuitive and smooth.

Mommy Fitness logo design

“Mommy Fitness” is a fitness brand and community tailored for mothers(pre, during, and post-pregnancy), promoting “strength in the middle of it”. I tried to design a logo for Mommy Fitness to create authentic, relatable, spunky, fun, healthy, and motivating.

The first idea is an object that combines shape of kettlebell and pacifier. Only part of the object shows up in the circle
make people think of either kettlebell or pacifier and strong woman’s hand hold the object. This concept
aims to appeal mommy to take care of her own health and fitness besides her baby.

In second design, instead of only showing part of the objects, this design tries to combine the images of kettlebell
or pacifier into one object by showing the whole object.

In third design, I use two simple lines to represent the curve waist of a fitness woman, while a navel with one line
represent the belly of pregnant woman. This concept aims to represent from pregnancy figure to a fitness
post pregnancy figure, which will motivate new mommy to take care of her own health and figure.
