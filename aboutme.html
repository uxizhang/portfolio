Designer seeking the essence of things

Hi, my name is Xi Zhang. I am currently a User Experience designer at GroupM in New York working on an AdTech SaaS product. I am the kind of designer who strives to see the world the way it really is but not the way we think it is. I believe by iteration of mockup and validation, design has the power to dig deep into complicated problems and solve the problems.

I also think design is the lubricant in a product team, I enjoy serving as a relator across the team and keenly support the whole product making procedure from concept to release.
