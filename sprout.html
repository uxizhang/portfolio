Sprout

A mobile app for new mothers to keep track of babies' daily and take care of themselves

About the project

This is a class project. Three User Experience design students at Pratt Institute designed a mobile app for new mothers from Jan, 2017 to May, 2017. The goal of this project was to seek opportunities to help new mothers to deal with their disordered life.

What I did

I worked on this project as an UX Designer. I was responsible for user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design.

New Mother's Life Needs To Be Saved

Jen gave birth to her first baby last month. She spends all time to take care of the baby. She feels sleepy and exhausted because she cannot sleep well during night, but she still needs to remember when to feed the baby, change diaper, get baby asleep...

New Mother Needs A Tool to Help With...

As a new mom, I would like to know what is the last time I feed the baby, so that I can know when I should feed the baby later.

As a new mom, I want the recording page of an app to be easy to access so that I can record when I am feeding the baby.

As a new mom, I would like to share record of baby’s daily routine with other family members, so that they can use it as a reference when they take care of the baby.

As a new mom, I would like to save photos and videos of the baby’s moment at one place, so that I can browse them easily.

EXPERIENCE MAP

New mother needs do lots of daily routines for her baby. We map out her journey, her thinking and feeling, and then we find our design opportunities.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

We mainly looked at how similar products in the market designed their homepages, feeding flow, and milestone flow. We compared what features they have and concluded their competitiveness. Combining with the opportunities we have in experience map, we have an idea of what features we would like include in our app.

A mobile app that new mothers could record baby's daily routine, get reminded of when to do what, access recording page easily, share logs and moments with others.

FEEDING FLOW

User is able to track the feeding progress in real time with the timer and save it as a record, as well as set the alarm for the next round feeding.
